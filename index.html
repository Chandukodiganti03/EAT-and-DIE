About EAD

Eat And Die (EAD) is your friendly neighborhood food delivery platform, bringing fresh meals from top restaurants directly to your doorstep. Our mission is to provide fast, safe, and delicious food to everyone.

Our Business

We partner with thousands of restaurants to offer a wide variety of cuisines, ensuring quality, convenience, and affordable prices. EAD is focused on leveraging technology for a seamless customer experience.

Delivery for Everyone

Our delivery network is fast and reliable, covering urban and semi-urban areas. Everyone can enjoy food from their favorite restaurants anytime.

News Room

Stay updated with the latest news and events at EAD. From new restaurant launches to exciting offers, our newsroom keeps you in the loop.

Sustainability

EAD is committed to eco-friendly practices, including sustainable packaging and reducing carbon footprint in deliveries, ensuring a healthier planet.

Investors

We work closely with investors to expand our reach and improve our services, aiming for growth that benefits both our partners and customers.

Contact Us

Have any questions or feedback? Reach out to us at support@eatanddie.com or call 1800-EDA-FOOD. We’re here to help!
